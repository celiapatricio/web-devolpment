Following the path of my parents, who are also Computer Engineers.

Family and Hometown

I come from a small town called El Escorial, located near Madrid, Spain. El Escorial is best known for its historic monastery, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that stands as a symbol of Spanish history and culture. Surrounded by mountains and peaceful landscapes, it is a place where tradition and nature meet.

Interests and Hobbies
